# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

100 Day Fitness is a local-first fitness app for home workout planning and a 100-day push-up challenge.

## Data Collection

100 Day Fitness does not require an account and does not collect personal data on an external server.

The app stores preferences and progress locally on the user's device, including:

- Selected language
- Selected color theme
- Selected difficulty
- Selected equipment
- Workout completion records
- Push-up challenge progress

This information stays on the device unless the user chooses to delete the app or clear app data.

## Health Data

100 Day Fitness does not connect to Apple Health, HealthKit, Google Fit, or Health Connect.

## Analytics And Tracking

100 Day Fitness does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Internet Links

Some exercise cards can open external image or video search links in the user's default browser. Those external websites are governed by their own privacy policies.
